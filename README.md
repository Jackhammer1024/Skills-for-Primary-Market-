# Skills for Primary Market

Codex skills for primary market investment workflows, including project judgment, diligence planning, interview minutes, evidence tracking, and IC memo synthesis.

## Skills

- `interview-qa-minutes`: Convert Chinese interview transcripts into formal Q&A-style investment meeting minutes.
- `primary-market-project-judgment`: Structure investment judgment using demand, value-chain position, competitive variables, company positioning, cycle stage, commercialization, risks, and valuation.
- `primary-market-diligence-planner`: Build focused diligence workplans for Chinese primary-market technology investments.
- `diligence-evidence-tracker`: Track facts, claims, risks, contradictions, follow-ups, and decision impact during diligence.
- `ic-memo-synthesizer`: Synthesize diligence findings into IC-ready investment memo outputs.
